JEREMY MCKINNON

Jeremy Wade McKinnon (born December 17, 1985) is an American musician and record producer, best known as a founding member and the lead vocalist of A Day to Remember

ALEX SHELNUTT

He joined A Day To Remember in 2006 shortly after the band signed with Victory Records. He replaced both Brandon Roberts and Bobby Scruggs, who previously played drums for the band.
